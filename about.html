Quality Assurance

PT. RF Hightek Indonesia quality system for products is ensured by employee involvement and participation in our quality system. It ensure the quality parameters meet the customer's requirements.

We are committed to furnishing RF and microwave components of the highest quality. Internal processes guarantee the effective controls necessary to produce a quality product and for evaluating performance and adherence to those processes.

The receipt and review of the customer's drawing and material list signals the start of quality for a product. It is reviewed for requirements and specifications necessary to perform and fulfill the contract. Quality requirements are noted and implemented on our internal work orders. Quality assurance items are coordinated with Engineering, Manufacturing, and Procurement during all phases of the life of the contract.
